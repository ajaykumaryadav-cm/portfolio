What is GEM Portal?

Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is a one-stop national procurement portal launched by the Government of India to facilitate online procurement of common use goods and services required by various government departments, organizations, and PSUs.

It brings transparency, efficiency, and speed in public procurement, making it mandatory for government agencies to procure goods and services through this digital platform.
